    About

    On Slack and GitHub, typing :heart: gives you ❤️. Mojito brings that same shortcut to every app on your Mac — Messages, Notes, your browser, your terminal, anywhere you type. Hit :, type a few letters, and pick from a little menu at your cursor. Or skip the menu and just close the colon: :heart: becomes the emoji on its own.

    Emoticons work too. :) turns into 🙂, <3 into ❤️, as you go. There's a similar shortcut for symbols you'd otherwise have to dig for: ::cmd:: for ⌘, ::option:: for ⌥, ::return:: for ↵. Mojito remembers your default skin tone, and stays out of the way in apps that already have their own emoji picker.

    Privacy

    Nothing leaves your Mac. Keystrokes are matched against the shortcode list locally; nothing is logged, transmitted, or stored. Password and other secure-text fields are ignored entirely. The only network request Mojito makes is (anonymously) checking for app updates.
