Guides

Everything about typing emoji, symbols, and GIFs on a Mac — the built-in ways macOS gives you, the keystrokes worth memorizing, and the fixes for when it all stops working.

Most of these guides answer their question with stock macOS alone. Where Mojito shows up, it's because one shortcode beats memorizing an Option-key chord: the app watches for a colon and autocompletes as you type, in any app. Three triggers cover everything:

::Symbols — every glyph macOS can draw::degree → °
:::GIFs, via Giphy, in any app:::excited → GIF panel
