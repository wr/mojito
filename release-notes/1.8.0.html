New

  • Insert several emoji in one go: hold Shift when picking — Shift+Return, Shift+Tab, or Shift+click — and the picker stays open for the next one. Type to search again, plain Return to insert and close. Works in the shortcode list and the full browser grid. (#163)
  • Custom aliases: map your own :word: to any emoji or symbol in Settings ▸ Aliases — they work alongside the built-in shortcodes. (#161)

Fixed

  • Per-site exclusions now match subdomains of plain URL patterns (example.com covers app.example.com). (#160)
  • Per-site exclusions now work in Arc, whose tab URL isn't exposed the usual way. (#159)
  • Shortcut recording in Settings ends cleanly when the window loses focus. (#162)
  • Pickers can no longer be covered by an app's own pop-up windows. (#155)
